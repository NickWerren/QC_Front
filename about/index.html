Quantized Crow is an online space created and run by Dr Nick Werren, a quantum physicist currently working at Heriot-Watt University. His research is focused on developing our understanding of biological mechanisms using the theory of open quantum systems and taking inspiration from biological systems in the development of new quantum technologies.

The core aim of Quantized Crow is to explore music, culture, and science through the posts seen in the other sections of the website. In particular, this site provides a space disentangled from the relentless noise of social media to produce and take in the work that is hosted here.

If you like it, then you can sign up to The Quantized Crow Newsletter.
